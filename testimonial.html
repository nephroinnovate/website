Success Story

My Journey with DialysisHub

When I was diagnosed with kidney failure in 2019, I felt overwhelmed by the prospect of dialysis treatments three times a week and the complicated medication schedule that came with it. Managing my health became a full-time job.

Since I started using DialysisHub, everything changed. The app helps me track my fluid intake, reminds me to take my medications, and lets me share my data instantly with my healthcare team. I've had fewer complications and hospital visits because problems are caught early.

What I appreciate most is how much independence I've gained. I feel like I have control over my condition rather than it controlling me.

James Wilson
Home Dialysis Patient
